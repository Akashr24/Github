How to Play

1

Click "Start Game" to begin your bingo adventure

2

Read the questions on each square - they're designed to reveal fun facts about people

3

Talk to people around you and find matches for each question

4

Tap squares when you find a match and watch your bingo card fill up

5

Get 5 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win!

Perfect for team building, networking events, parties, and icebreakers. No personal data stored - just fun connections!
